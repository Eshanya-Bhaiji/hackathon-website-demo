The Initiative

Born from a vision to fortify our digital borders, CyberShield began as a specialized research group. Today, we have evolved into a national-level platform where ethical hackers, developers, and security analysts converge to solve critical infrastructure challenges.

We believe in proactive defense. Every challenge in this hackathon is an opportunity to learn, secure, and deliver excellence in the face of evolving global threats.

🎯

Our Mission

To cultivate a high-caliber cybersecurity workforce through hands-on challenges that secure our digital future.

💡

Our Values

Integrity, continuous technical evolution, and a focus on building resilient, user-centric security protocols.

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick intelligence for participants and partners.

How do I register my team?

Click the "Register Now" button in the navigation bar. You will need to provide team details and a brief summary of your expertise.

Who is eligible to participate?

We welcome university students, independent researchers, and industry professionals. If you have a passion for security, you belong in the shield.

What is the duration of the hackathon?

The main event is a 36-hour continuous sprint. We provide a full roadmap and technical mentor support throughout the duration.

Will hardware be provided for the IoT track?

Standard simulation environments are provided. For specific hardware-based solutions, teams are encouraged to bring their own kits or request them 48 hours in advance.
